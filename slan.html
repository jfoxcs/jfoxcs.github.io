Why Nakisa?

After several years of co-development between SAP & Nakisa, the SAP Lease Administration by Nakisa (SLAN) solution has finally arrived.

Here's what you can look forward to with the new solution:

Why Now?

Most organizations - both lessors and lessees - will be impacted by the new and upcoming regulatory compliance standards.

⦁ ASC 842 effective: December 15, 2018

⦁ IFRS 16 effective: January 1 , 2019

As current administration standards change, it's imperative that all affected organizations ensure they're compliant. This can be a large undertaking for many businesses, as leasing data may be handled by several different departments. Now is the best time to prepare your organization & make the leap to SLAN.
